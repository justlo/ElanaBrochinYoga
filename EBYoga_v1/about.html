about elana

Elana started doing yoga as a way to reduce stress and anxiety in 2008. As a former marathon runner, Elana found it difficult to stay still for traditional meditation and found the active nature of yoga more conducive to her energetic lifestyle. After practicing for a short time, Elana discovered that yoga, in addition to a mental exercise, was also a fun new physical challenge. She especially loves working on arm balances and handstands. Elana also found yoga to be a good complement to running, allowing her to bring more awareness to her body. After years of practice, in 2013 Elana decided to train to be a yoga instructor though the Charlestown Yoga School's 200 hour teacher training. Elana continues to develop her own personal practice and also teaches "slow flow and restorative" class at Hebrew SeniorLife.
